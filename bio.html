BIO:
⚔️ © bariscavusoglu
Barış Çavuşoğlu b. 1994, is an animator and visual artist.
They studied 3D VFX at Escape Studios London and went on to
study Computer Science with Games Technology BSc at City,
University of London. In 2018 they moved to Berlin and became
a part of Random Collective Berlin, an organization focusing
on animation and design. They are now living and working in
Los Angeles where they are developing a singular and loud
animation style of their own fusing grotesque cartooning,
and surreal black comedy in the form of XR, video art and
installations. They are currently an Experimental Animation
MFA candidate at California Institute of the Arts.
Between exhibitions and personal projects, they also worked
as a freelance animation director for Adult Swim. Their
animations have been screened internationally at the
likes of Annecy International Animation Film Festival,
REDCAT Theatre, OVNi Festival France, Lithuanian International
Shorts and Dutch Design Week. In November 2021 they opened a
dual presentation at BSMNT Leipzig, Germany with Kid Xanthrax
called #SLIPGNOSIS
